# Getting Started

Welcome to Muzakily, your personal music streaming application.

## First Login

1. Navigate to your Muzakily instance in a web browser
2. Enter your email and password provided by your administrator
3. Click **Login**

After logging in, you'll see the main interface with your music library.

## Interface Overview

### Sidebar

The sidebar provides navigation to all main sections:

- **Home** - Dashboard with recent activity
- **Search** - Find songs, albums, and artists
- **Songs** - Browse all songs
- **Albums** - Browse by album
- **Artists** - Browse by artist
- **Playlists** - Your playlists

Below the navigation, you'll see your playlists for quick access.

### Main Content Area

The center of the screen shows content for the selected section. This is where you browse your library, view album details, manage playlists, and more.

### Player Bar

The bottom bar contains the audio player:

- **Now Playing** - Shows current song with artwork
- **Playback Controls** - Play/pause, previous, next
- **Progress Bar** - Seek within the song
- **Volume Control** - Adjust volume
- **Queue** - View and manage upcoming songs
- **Device Picker** - Switch playback between devices

## Playing Music

### Play a Song

1. Navigate to any song list (Songs, Album, Playlist, etc.)
2. Click on a song to start playing
3. The player bar appears at the bottom

### Queue Management

- **Play Next**: Right-click a song and select "Play Next"
- **Add to Queue**: Right-click and select "Add to Queue"
- **View Queue**: Click the queue icon in the player bar
- **Reorder**: Drag and drop songs in the queue
- **Remove**: Click the X next to a queued song

### Playback Controls

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Space | Play/Pause |
| ← | Previous song |
| → | Next song |
| ↑ | Volume up |
| ↓ | Volume down |
| M | Mute/Unmute |

## Browsing Your Library

### Songs View

Shows all songs in your library. Use the controls at the top to:

- **Search** - Filter by title, artist, or album
- **Sort** - Order by title, artist, album, year, or date added
- **Filter** - Show only favorites, specific genres, or formats

### Albums View

Browse albums with cover art. Click an album to see its tracks.

### Artists View

Browse artists alphabetically. Click an artist to see their albums and songs.

## Favorites

Mark songs, albums, artists, or playlists as favorites:

1. Hover over any item
2. Click the heart icon
3. The item appears in your Favorites section

Access favorites from the sidebar or use the filter in song listings.

## Settings

Access settings from the user menu (top right):

- **Audio Quality** - Choose streaming quality
- **Theme** - Light or dark mode
- **Notifications** - Enable/disable alerts
- **Account** - Update password

## Mobile Access

Muzakily works in mobile browsers. For the best experience:

1. Open your Muzakily URL in Safari or Chrome
2. Add to Home Screen for app-like access
3. The interface adapts to your screen size

## Getting Help

If you encounter issues:

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Contact your administrator
